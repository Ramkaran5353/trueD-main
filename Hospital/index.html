Our Speciality's

Bone Surgery

Bone surgery, also known as orthopedic surgery is a medical specialty that focuses on the diagnosis ,treatment, and prevention of disorders and injuries affecting the musculoskeletal system.

Knee Surgery

Knee surgery, also known as knee arthroscopy, is a surgical procedure that uses a small camera to visualize the inside of the knee joint to diagnose and treat knee conditions.

Leg surgery

Leg surgery treats disorders, injuries, or conditions affecting the legs, ankles, and feet to restore function, alleviate pain, and improve mobility.

Tendon Surgery

Tendon surgery is a procedure that repairs or reconstructs damaged or torn tendons to restore normal function and alleviate symptoms.
